About Anonymous Secrets

Welcome to Anonymous Secrets, the simple and privacy-focused platform for sharing your thoughts and secrets without revealing who you are. Whether you need to vent, confess, or just let something off your chest, you can do so here in complete anonymity.

How It Works

Submit a secret by typing into the box on the home page. Secrets appear instantly for everyone to see, and you can browse the latest 10 secrets or discover a random one with a click.

Privacy & Data Retention

We value your privacy. We do not track IP addresses, user accounts, or any personal identifiers. All secrets are stored temporarily and automatically deleted after 30 days, ensuring that no long-term record is kept.

Nothing to Lose, Everything to Share

There’s no sign-up, no tracking scripts, and no hidden logs. Your messages are yours to share and ours to protect—then to delete after 30 days. Enjoy the freedom of true anonymity.
